

RESTful Interface:

GET http://[hostname]/andyBee/api/v1.0/databases
  Retrieve a list of databases

GET http://[hostname]/andyBee/api/v1.0/databases/[db_name]
  Retrieve a list of caches for the given database

GET http://[hostname]/andyBee/api/v1.0/databases/[db_name]/[cache_id]
  Retrieve cache details


GET http://[hostname]/andyBee/api/v1.0/config
  Retrieves a config profile

PUT http://[hostname]/andyBee/api/v1.0/config
